# Human Resources Management

**Concept of Human Resources Management:**

- Human Resources Management (HRM) is the function of managing an organization's employees or human capital.
- The goal of HRM is to ensure that the organizationâ€™s employees are efficient, productive, and happy in their roles.
- HRM covers a range of activities, including recruitment and selection of employees, training and development, performance management, compensation and benefits, and employee relations.
- HRM plays a crucial role in the success of any organization by creating a healthy work environment and culture that fosters growth and innovation within the company.
- Well-managed HRM systems can also help establish and maintain competitive advantage and increase the overall value of the organization.

In summary, HRM is a critical function in any organization that focuses on managing the company's most important asset - its employees - to achieve optimal business results.
